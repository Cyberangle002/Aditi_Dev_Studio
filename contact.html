Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website takes 2-3 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

What are your payment terms?

We typically follow a 50% advance and 50% on completion structure. For larger projects, we can discuss milestone-based payments.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! All our projects include post-launch support. We offer various maintenance packages to keep your digital assets running smoothly.

Can you work with my existing brand?

Absolutely! We can work with your existing brand guidelines or help you create new ones. We're flexible and adapt to your needs.

Do you sign NDA agreements?

Yes, we're happy to sign NDAs and confidentiality agreements. Your project's privacy and security are our top priorities.
